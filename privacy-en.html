This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is processed in the mobile app Little Linu (the “App”).

1. Data Controller

[Fabian Schmid]
[Germany]

Email: littlelinuteam@yahoo.com

2. Overview

Little Linu is an app for expectant and new parents. You can create a user account, manage child profiles, use checklists and custom lists, keep a diary, and read informational articles. Your data is stored primarily in your account and—if you invite family members—shared with authorized members of the same child profile.

We do not sell your data and do not show personalized advertising in the App.

3. Data We Process

3.1 User Account and Profile

When you register and use the App, we process in particular:

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted by our authentication provider; we do not store it in plain text)
  • Nickname / display name
  • Selected profile picture (avatar)
  • Country
  • Preferred language (German / English)

3.2 Child Profiles

For each child profile you create, you may store among other things:

  • Child’s name
  • Date of birth (or relevant date for pregnancy/development features)
  • Profile picture (avatar)
  • Settings for name visibility to invited members
  • Invitation code for other users to join

3.3 Content You Create

Depending on how you use the App, we may also process:

  • Checklists, topics, tasks, comments, and assignments
  • Custom lists and list items
  • Diary entries (text, category, date, author display)
  • Saved articles (bookmarks)
  • Memberships and invitations to child profiles

3.4 Technical and Security Data

  • Session and authentication information (locally on your device and with our backend provider)
  • For password reset: email address, time-limited verification code (stored hashed), log data for abuse prevention
  • When errors occur in the App: technical error and diagnostic data via our monitoring service (see Section 5)

3.5 Contact

If you contact us by email (e.g. via “Message our team”), we process the information you provide (email address, message content).

3.6 No Special Categories of Personal Data

The App is not designed to deliberately collect special categories of personal data within the meaning of Art. 9 GDPR (e.g. health data in the strict legal sense). Diary or note content is provided voluntarily by you.

6. Sharing with Other Users

Data in a child profile (e.g. checklists, diary, lists) is visible to invited and authorized members of that profile where the App features allow. We do not share data with unrelated third parties unless you contact us directly or we are legally required to do so.

7. Retention Period

  • Account data and content: until you or authorized members delete your account or the respective child profile.
  • Password reset codes: short-term (approx. 10 minutes), then invalid; technical logs only as long as needed for security and abuse prevention.
  • Error reports (Sentry): according to Sentry configuration and retention periods (typically limited).
  • Support emails: until the request is resolved, at most as required by statutory retention periods.

8. Account Deletion

You can delete your account in the App (“Delete account”). Your user account and associated personal data will be largely removed, unless legal retention obligations apply. Shared child profiles may—depending on your role— transfer to other members or be deleted (see in-app function and deletion notices).

You have the following rights vis-à-vis the data controller, among others:

  • Access (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Objection to processing based on legitimate interests (Art. 21 GDPR)

To exercise your rights, contact: littlelinuteam@yahoo.com.

10. Withdrawal of Consent

Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time. The lawfulness of processing before withdrawal remains unaffected.

11. Right to Lodge a Complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority—particularly in the EU member state of your habitual residence, place of work, or place of the alleged infringement.

In Germany, for example, the supervisory authority for your place of residence. Overview: bfdi.bund.de.

12. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data—including encrypted transmission (HTTPS/TLS), database-level access controls (Row Level Security), and role-based sharing within child profiles.

Absolute security cannot be guaranteed for internet services. Please choose a strong password and do not share your invitation code with unauthorized persons.

13. Children

The App is intended for adult users (especially parents and caregivers). User accounts are not intended for children under 16. Information about children (e.g. name, date of birth) is entered by parents, guardians, or authorized users.

14. Obligation to Provide Data

Providing personal data is generally required to use the App where a feature depends on it (e.g. email for registration). Without such data, the relevant features cannot be offered.

15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the App, legal requirements, or services change. The current version is available at the URL we publish. For material changes, we will inform you in the App or by email where possible.
